A Personal Accomplishment

One of my most recent personal accomplishments was vending at the “Spring on the Bluff” at the farmhouse on Green Bluff. Working as a vendor hasn’t been successful for me in the past. From bad weather to holiday weekends, I struggled to find success at physical events. However, this was different. Not only did this event feel different from the beginning but I was more prepared and more excited. I ended the evening with almost no stock left and a full heart. Perhaps this is because of my more developed customer service, the event being worked, the gorgeous weather or some combination but regardless my heart was full.
